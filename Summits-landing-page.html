Summits

Unique events • Leading Experts • Immersive experiences

92Y Summits leverage 92Y’s cultural capital to bring you the leading thinkers and most in-demand experts on any given topic, creating world-class programming on a fully immersive, interactive platform.

Ideas are alive and evolving. Approaches to the most significant topics are constantly changing. 92Y Summits provide unparalleled opportunities to take a deep dive into the topics and ideas that deserve a closer look. We bring together experts from all over the world to share their learning, perspective and point of view.

Future of Democracy Summit

April 2021

Following the most tumultuous and disruptive election season in recent American history, this summit convenes politicians from across the spectrum with analysts, journalists, policy experts, scholars, activists, and public servants to explore democracy and how to preserve it. From media influence to civic participation to government function, where does democracy go from here?

Summit for Life

November 2021
